Amazon Digital Product Tester (Remote / No Experience)

8/24/2023

 
Amazon offers remote opportunities for Digital Product Testers with no prior experience required. As a Digital Product Tester, you'll have the exciting role of testing and providing feedback on various digital products and services offered by Amazon. This position enables you to work from the comfort of your home while gaining hands-on experience with cutting-edge technologies. Amazon values diverse perspectives, making it an excellent opportunity for individuals looking to start their career in the digital industry. Join Amazon's team of Digital Product Testers and play a crucial role in shaping the future of their digital offerings.

What You Will Be Doing
This job involves testing out various products found on Amazon and other platforms. You will download books, movies, apps, and software to try out the various features. Then you will write up a short review to tell the world what you thought of the product. You can make up to $272 a day in this role, and there is no limit to the amount of work available.
Rate: $34 per hour
Requirements: You need to have access to either an iPhone/iPad or Android Phone/Tablet.  You also need to have a reliable internet connection and have at least basic English writing skills. 
Skills/background needed: These are entry-level writing jobs, so full training is provided. You do not need to have done any paid writing work before. 
Location: You can work remotely online from United States. We are particularly keen to recruit writers from your area.
Paid reviewers are in huge demand worldwide right now. So if you can start right away, please apply below.
