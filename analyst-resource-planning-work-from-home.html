Analyst - Resource Planning - Work From Home

8/24/2023

 
​Job Description

As an Analyst of Strategic Planning at CVS Health you will be part of a team who are responsible for the long-term forecasting, budgeting, and resource planning for multiple lines of business. The team builds and manages the forecasts and budgets that drive our business results and supports more than 9 million members and hundreds of clients.

The team will be building long term forecasts and resource plans which determine the need for our overall production and non-production staffing. Forecasting for a variety of clients, lines of business, and sources.

The accuracy of the strategic forecast is a key driver to the operations team abilities to provide timely decisions to members, prescribers, and clients within the forecasted spend. The team will be supporting the operation teams as they work across multiple clients, lines of business and sources. By providing accurate strategic forecast the operations team will be able to meet client expectations to avoid risk associated with performance guarantees.

Responsibilities will include:

  • Providing guidance to operational leaders and help develop an overall strategic strategy that will drive optimal outcomes.
  • This role will help create forecasted volume, resources and non-production needs that drive compliance for timeliness of work and determine an optimal systems solution that will support all workforce activities.
  • This analyst will help create and support staffing contingency and business continuity plans in anticipation of issues that may arise.
  • Using statistical tools to identify, analyze, and interpret patterns and trends in complex data sets could be helpful for the diagnosis and prediction.
  • Preparing reports for management stating trends, patterns, and predictions using relevant data.
  • Preparing reports for the stakeholders to understand the data-analysis steps, enabling them to take important decisions based on various facts and trends.
  • To be successful in this role you will be partnering with operations, team lead, and Senior Leadership to understand the various partner’s needs and help coordinate the actions needed to fulfill those needs.
